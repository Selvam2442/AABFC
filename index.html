Welcome to Arokiya Annai Blessed Flair Center

Welcome

AABFC Educational Council was established under the Constitution of India and incorporated under the legislation to promote vocational education and flair development. During historical educational commissions, it was insisted that manual and productive work should not only be an integral part of education but should center round it.

Education is an endless process needed for the development of any nation. India being one of the developing countries concentrates on youth education as a maximum percent of youth are in our country. A better educated labour force is essential if we are to meet the supply requirements of faster growth.

Education improves functional and analytical ability and thereby opens up opportunities for individuals and also groups to achieve greater access to labour markets and livelihoods. Skills and knowledge are the engines of economic growth and social development of any country. Countries with higher and better levels of knowledge and skills respond more effectively and promptly to challenges and opportunities of globalization.
